8.6 Screen distances

A screen distance can be specified in different ways depending on the distance unit you want to use:

  • float: a distance in screen pixels.

  • a pair float#c: a distance in centimeters.

  • a pair float#i: a distance in inches.

  • a pair float#m: a distance in millimeters.

  • a pair float#p: a distance in picas.
